About Me

This is who I am.

I'm an anti social, middle aged man with good manners and a general distrust in other human beings. I have met some truly wonderful people over the years, but let's be honest, most of you suck.
I have children, a wife and a mountain of debt to accompany it. I drive a 20 year old Citreon and have the life experience of a someone 100 years older than me. I was a successful Software Engineer for many years until I finally burned out (or had a breakdown, the jury is still out), and now I write posts for internet strangers hoping it will afford me food and rent.

My childhood was too colourful to put on the internet, but let's just say I grew up fast. I was booted from school at 13, spent a few years either on the dole or working awful jobs, then clawed my way to a University Degree in my late 20's and have done everything from unemployed day drinking in a bus stop, to giving presentations on how to save a company millions in revenue.

My personality and interests are quite varied, I can be a mega nerd and spend hours building a PC or gaming, but then I'm also comfortable plastering a wall, digging up the garden or sparring with the lads.

For fun I like to play guitar, video games or movies/shows, and when I can be bothered: exercise! I ride a motorcycle to keep my sanity (or escape my children), I like boxing and used to do it myself, but dislike all other sports. Yes, that's right, watching millionaires chasing bags of wind does not entertain me.

In general I like to think I am (as my son would say), a chill guy.
