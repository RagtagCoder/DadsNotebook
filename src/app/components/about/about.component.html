About Me

This is who I am.

First the basics: I'm an anti social, middle aged British guy with great manners but a general distrust in other humans. I've met some truly wonderful people over the years, but let's be honest here, most of you suck.
I have children, a wife and a mountain of debt. I drive a car I'm almost ashamed of and the life experience of a 140 year old. I was a Software Engineer for 10 years until I finally burned out, and now I write posts where I bitch and moan about whatever first world problem happens to be getting under my skin.

My childhood was too colourful to put on an about me page, but let's just say I grew up in poverty with a murderous father, clawed my way to a University Degree in my late 20's and have done everything from day drinking in a bus stop in my tracksuit, to giving presentations to board members on how to save them millions of pounds per annum.

For fun I like to play my guitar, piano or PS5, I ride a motorcycle to keep my sanity (or escape my children) and I don't drink alcohol or take drugs.
